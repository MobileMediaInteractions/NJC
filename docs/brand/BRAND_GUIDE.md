# The New Jersey Courier brand guide

## Core identity

**Name:** The New Jersey Courier
**Conversational name:** NJ Courier
**Tagline:** The Authoritative Voice of the Garden State
**Launch edition:** Middlesex County
**Launch origin:** Vercel-generated `*.vercel.app` HTTPS alias
**Planned primary domain:** `njcourier.com`
**Initial asset origin:** same-origin `/assets`
**Planned optional asset origin:** `cdn.njcourier.com`

The identity should feel established without pretending to be old, civic without becoming governmental, and distinctly New Jersey without relying on state-outline clichés everywhere. The masthead is editorial and serif-led; navigation, data, labels and product controls use a quiet modern sans serif.

## Logo system

- Use the full masthead at wide desktop sizes, in press materials and on social cards.
- Use the NJC seal in app icons, favicons, avatars and compact navigation.
- Keep at least one-half the seal width as clear space around the seal.
- Never stretch, outline, rotate, add shadows to or recolor the marks outside the approved palette.
- Do not place the full wordmark below 280 CSS pixels wide; use the text masthead or NJC seal instead.

## Color

| Token | Hex | Purpose |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Courier Forest | `#173E32` | Primary masthead, navigation, dark surfaces |
| Civic Green | `#2F6654` | Links, informational accents |
| Turnpike Gold | `#C49545` | Rules, highlights, awards and interaction emphasis |
| Courier Red | `#A5382F` | Breaking and urgent states only |
| Newsprint | `#F8F5EE` | Primary light background |
| Warm Paper | `#F6F1E7` | Illustrations and inverse text |
| Ink | `#171917` | Body text |

Gold is an accent, not a body-text color. Red is reserved for genuinely urgent or breaking information and must not be used as a general promotional color.

## Typography

- **Editorial headlines and masthead:** Newsreader, with Georgia as the fallback.
- **Interface and body copy:** Geist Sans.
- **Metrics, API keys and technical values:** Geist Mono.

Headlines use restrained negative tracking and generous line height. The product should feel spacious: fewer simultaneous modules, stronger hierarchy and wider gutters than a traditional metropolitan newspaper homepage.

## Editorial imagery

Reported news photography must be documentary, accurately captioned and credited. AI-generated imagery may be used only for clearly labeled brand, opinion or conceptual artwork—not as evidence of an event. The included Garden State engraving is a generated brand illustration and must retain that classification in the asset manifest.

## Voice

Write with local specificity, direct verbs and useful context. Avoid institutional self-importance, partisan shorthand and broadcast-style hype. “Authoritative” is earned through sourcing, corrections, transparency and subject knowledge.
